Hello! I'm Shunzi Lu (pictured on the right).

I am a PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania studying Physical Oceanography in the Department of Earth & Environmental Science, and I work in the Oceans and Climate Group.

My research focuses on understanding the dynamics of the Earth's climate system. I am particularly interested in the Southern Ocean, a crucial region that plays a key role in regulating global climate patterns, and how advanced climate models capture these complex processes, aiming to improve our understanding of climate behavior and its implications for the future.
